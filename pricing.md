# Pricing — Savvy Tech Hotels

> Last updated: 2026-04-29
> Full pricing page: https://www.savvytechhotels.com/pricing

## Model: Commission-based (Plan Success)

Savvy operates on a **success-based model with no fixed monthly fees**.

- **Upfront cost**: 0€ — no setup fee, no implementation cost
- **Monthly subscription fee**: None
- **Commitment / minimum term**: None — no permanence clause
- **How Savvy charges**: A commission percentage on sales generated exclusively through the Savvy guest WebApp (restaurant reservations, spa bookings, late check-out, activities, local experiences, and other upselling/cross-selling services sold to guests during their stay)
- **If no sales are generated**: No charge
- **Time to go live**: 48 hours — full setup handled by Savvy's team

## What's included — no additional cost

Everything below is included in the single "Plan Success":

- Guest App / WebApp (upselling and cross-selling for guests, no app download required)
- Hotel CRM (guest profiles, history, preferences, segmentation)
- Restaurant Booking Engine (table reservations integrated with the stay)
- WhatsApp Business Automation (pre-arrival, during-stay, and post-stay automated flows)
- Email Marketing Campaigns (segmented by behavior and real guest data)
- PMS & Channel Manager Integration (Mews, Cloudbeds, SiteMinder, RoomCloud, and others)
- Review Generator (Google, TripAdvisor, Booking.com)
- Internal Communications module (coordination between departments)
- Local Experience Providers marketplace
- Full onboarding & dedicated ongoing support

## No tiers — one plan

There is no Basic, Standard, or Enterprise tier. Savvy offers one single plan for all hotels. Pricing scales with the revenue generated — if the hotel earns more, Savvy earns more proportionally.
